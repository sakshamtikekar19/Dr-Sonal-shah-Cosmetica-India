About Dr. Sonal R. Shah

BHMS, PGDCC | Co-owner of Cosmetica India Academy | Skin Care | Hair Care | General Health | Homeopathy — 26 years of experience

Dr Sonal is an experienced Homeopathic Physician and Skin Care Consultant based in Dombivli, with over two decades of clinical practice. She is the founder of Dr Sonal Shah Cosmetica India, a trusted name known for patient-centric care and ethical medical practice.

She holds a BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery), PGDCC degree, and is a registered practitioner under the Maharashtra Council of Homeopathy. She is also the co-owner of Cosmetica India Academy. Over the years, Dr Sonal has successfully treated a wide range of skin, hair, and general health concerns through a balanced approach that combines medical knowledge, modern aesthetic procedures, and individualized treatment planning.

Dr Sonal has been practicing skin and aesthetic care for the past twelve years, having successfully treated 1000+ patients — and the numbers are still rising every day. Beyond the clinic, she conducts training at Cosmetica India Academy, teaching new doctors about therapies and treatments, and regularly holds seminars to share her expertise with the medical community.

At Dr Sonal Shah Cosmetica India, Dr Sonal’s focus is on understanding the root cause of each condition rather than offering one-size-fits-all solutions. Patients appreciate her calm approach, clear explanations, and commitment to long-term results — whether for acne management, hair concerns, skin procedures, or overall wellness.

Areas of Expertise Include:

  • Acne & Acne Scar Management
  • Skin Growth Removal (Moles, Warts, Skin Tags)
  • Laser-based Skin & Hair Treatments
  • Hair Loss & PRP Therapy
  • General Medical & Homeopathic Consultation

Dr Sonal believes that effective treatment begins with trust, transparency, and personalized care — values that define every consultation at Dr Sonal Shah Cosmetica India.
